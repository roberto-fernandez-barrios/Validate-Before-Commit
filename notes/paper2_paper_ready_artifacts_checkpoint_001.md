## Status

The controlled streaming experiment is now frozen as the main experimental block for Paper 2.

Main claim:
Certain quantum kernel geometries, especially PauliXZ and ZZ, provide stronger streaming drift sensitivity than a classical RBF-MMD baseline under controlled benign-only temporal drift, especially from moderate severity onward.

Important limitation:
The evidence does not support universal quantum superiority. The contribution is regime-aware and operational: quantum kernel geometry can provide earlier/more sensitive drift signals under specific monitored shift regimes.
