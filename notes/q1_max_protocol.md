## Registered deltas vs PLAN_Q1_CLAUDE_CODE (with rationale, BEFORE running)

**D1 — P0.1 gets a third, theory-first leg — under Roberto's precision (2026-07-19): the
weak-null guarantee may be attributed to the current accumulate-across-windows variant ONLY
if the full formal chain closes.** Required, in this order, before any such claim enters the
manuscript: (i) formal definition of the null, H0_cond: E[d_t | F_{t-1}] ≤ 0 for all t,
where d_t is the per-flow paired correctness difference and F_{t-1} the filtration
generated by everything observed before flow t (per class, on the class substream); (ii)
identification of the EXACT test supermartingale / e-process generated by
`cs_lower_bound_eb`: W_n = exp(Σ_i λ_i (x_i − 1/2) − Σ_i ψ_E(λ_i) (x_i − μ̂_{i−1})²) with
x = (d+1)/2, predictable λ_i (capped at 3/4) and predictable μ̂_{i−1}, and the exact
code–theorem correspondence `cs_lower_bound_eb(d, α) > 0  ⟺  W_n > 1/α`; (iii) proof that
W_n is a nonnegative supermartingale under H0_cond with VARIABLE conditional means — via
Fan's inequality exp(λy − ψ_E(λ)y²) ≤ 1 + λy for y ≥ −1, λ ∈ [0,1), giving
E[increment | F] ≤ (1 + λ(μ_t − μ̂)) e^{λ(μ̂ − 1/2)} ≤ e^{λ(μ_t − 1/2)} ≤ 1 whenever
μ_t ≤ 1/2 — written out in the supplement, checked line-by-line against the implementation
(predictability of λ_i and μ̂, cap < 1, y > −1, α fixed at proposal time); (iv) null
SIMULATIONS with time-varying conditional means (all ≤ 0, e.g. oscillating and trending)
AND time-varying variances (tie probability changing across blocks), confirming
false-commit ≤ α, as a mandatory test (`test_weak_null_drifting_means`). If ANY of
(i)–(iv) fails to close, the accumulate variant is demoted to HEURISTIC ABLATION with no
stated guarantee; Cohort is then the guaranteed variant and Refresh the operational one.
Even if it closes, the paper states the weak-null target explicitly ("the challenger is at
no time conditionally better on the probe stream" — rejecting it does not establish
superiority at commit time), which is why Cohort/Refresh are offered regardless. Standard
argument (testing-by-betting); cited, not claimed as novel.

## Hypotheses (registered)

H1 (P1.1): there exists a lifetime-budgeted configuration at cap ≤1024 meeting the
non-vacuity target in PortScan-full. Prediction: pooled EB-CS + defer + Bonferroni reaches
it at cap 256–512; the maximally-stacked stratified+pseries variant does not before 1024.
H2 (P0.1): Cohort ≈ current-defer in zero drift (both 0 commits); Refresh commits slightly
less than current-defer under gradual drift (evidence resets); no variant produces harmful
commits in zero drift.
H3 (P0.3): inversion replicates at 130 seeds; own_transformer TOST-equivalent at ±0.5;
independent not TOST-equivalent at ±1.0 (fallback wording pre-agreed).
H4 (P1.3): CICIDS incumbents collapse in every new cross-day replay (deep benefit);
healthy-incumbent chronological behaviour remains confined to UNSW timelines; if any new
stream shows net harm we report it as the first chronological harm observation (and do NOT
re-run it under alternative configs to strengthen it).
H5 (P1.4): alert-enriched acquisition needs ~1/π fewer inspected flows than random for the
same adjudicated labels; gate-vs-naive ordering unchanged by acquisition policy where the
trigger fires.
